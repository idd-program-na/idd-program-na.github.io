What To Expect

  • Year 1

    Introducing all paths of Interaction Design which include Information Architecture, Usability Testing, 3D Modelling and Graphic Design. By combining their skills from all courses, students learn the process of building a prototype from the ideation process to creating an interactive product.

  • Year 2

    With experience in creating a product from the ground up, students begin portfolio building with a variety of projects, i.e. 3D Modeling, brand and marketing for a company and a web interfaces.

  • Year 3

    Students in their final year use all previous knowledge to develop a senior thesis after given a design brief.

  • Optional 7th Semester

    Optional Co-Op Education Course where students are able to experience working in the industry environment and work with industry professionals.
